How do I make a return?
  1. Go to "My Orders" and select the items you want to return.
  2. Complete the return request with your order number and correct info.
  3. Drop your package at the nearest Aramex Office or wait for pickup.
  4. Aramex will pick it up within 3 business days. LCW pays the cost.
  5. Track it via Aramex website or in My Orders page.
  6. To get your invoice:
    • Log in to your profile
    • Click on My Orders
    • Select the order
    • Click on E-Archive Invoice
  7. You'll receive a copy of the return request. To track:
    • Log in to your profile
    • Click on My Orders
    • Select the return order
  8. You can return online orders in LCW stores.
Stores Return Policy
  • Maximum of 10 items can be returned in store.
  • More than 10 items: use online return or return on different days.
  • Discounted products can be returned in store.
  • Refunded amount = net amount after discount.
  • Items bought from other sites can't be returned in store.
  • Defective/missing items must be returned online.
  • LC Money + cash orders refunded in cash.
  • Refunds made using original payment method.
